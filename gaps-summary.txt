ARCNAVE — Current Gaps Summary

Modules 0–8 are complete as planned vertical slices, but the product still has documented functional and production gaps.

Highest priority before or during Module 9

- Notification ledger missing: required before AI can draft/send discretionary email, SMS, or WhatsApp. Current email only supports staff activation after human approval.
- Document storage not deployment-safe: no Docker volume for uploads, no file-backup plan, and no encryption at rest.
- No background-job system: needed for bulk OCR, imports, and notifications.
- No initial platform-admin bootstrap: a fresh deployment needs a direct database insert to create its first super admin.
- Password reset remains a 501 stub. Principal invites return a raw token instead of delivering email and have no resend/revoke flow.
- Module 9 itself is unbuilt: Tool Registry, Context Builder, Prompt Safety Layer, RAG, and governed AI API.

Functional gaps

- Scholarship eligibility is blocked because students lack annual_income.
- Academic timetable CSV import and raw timetable-file storage are absent.
- Reports currently only provide student export; attendance and finance reports are absent.
- OCR extraction is not built.
- No enforced uniqueness for one Principal per college or one HOD per department.
- Departments are only partly normalized; existing staff and academic department fields remain free text.

Note

Several older Module 1, 2, and 5 notes saying "requires Module 8" are outdated because Module 8 is now built.
