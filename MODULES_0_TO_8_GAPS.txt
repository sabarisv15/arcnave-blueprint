ARCNAVE - Modules 0 to 8: Gap Summary

Modules 0-8 are complete as their planned vertical slices, but these
product and production gaps remain.

Highest priority before or during Module 9

- Notification ledger missing: required before AI can draft/send
  discretionary email, SMS, or WhatsApp. Current email only supports
  staff activation after human approval.
- Document storage is not deployment-safe: no Docker volume for
  uploaded files, no file backup plan, and no encryption at rest.
- No background-job system: needed for bulk OCR, imports, and
  notifications.
- No initial platform-admin bootstrap: a fresh deployment needs a
  direct database insert to create its first super admin.
- Password reset remains a 501 stub; principal invites return a raw
  token instead of delivering an email and have no resend/revoke flow.
- Module 9 itself: no Tool Registry, Context Builder, Prompt Safety
  Layer, RAG, or governed AI API yet.

Functional gaps

- Scholarship eligibility is blocked because students lack
  annual_income.
- Academic timetable CSV import and raw timetable-file storage are
  absent.
- Reports currently provide only student export; attendance and finance
  reports are absent.
- No OCR extraction yet.
- No enforced uniqueness for one Principal per college or one HOD per
  department.
- Departments are only partly normalized; existing staff and academic
  department fields remain free text.

Notes

- Several older Module 1, 2, and 5 notes that say "requires Module 8"
  are now outdated because Module 8 is built.
